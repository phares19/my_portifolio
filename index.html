About Me

Four Passions. One Person.

I don't fit into one box — and that's what makes me unique.

I'm a Mechanical Engineering student with a deep love for understanding how things work — from engines to algorithms. Outside the classroom, I produce music beats that I've never published (yet), analyze Forex markets for short-term trades, and build web applications that solve real problems.

Each discipline feeds the others: engineering gives me systematic thinking, music gives me creativity, trading gives me risk management and discipline, and programming gives me the power to build anything. I'm not famous — yet. But I'm building every single day.
